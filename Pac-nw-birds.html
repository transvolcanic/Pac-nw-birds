Common Birds of the Pacific Northwest by their habitats

There are many diverse bird habitats in the Pacific Northwest.

Listed here are some of those habitats and some of the birds that inhabit them.

  • Pelagic

    • Sooty Shearwater
    • Parasitic Jaeger
    • Northern Fulmar

  • Coastal

    • Pelagic Cormorant
    • Surf Scoter
    • Sanderling

  • Wetland

    • American Bittern
    • Green-winged Teal
    • Marsh Wren

  • Lowlands and Riparian Forest

    • Black-headed Grosbeak
    • Red-breasted Sapsucker
    • Bewick's Wren

  • Alpine Forest

    • Hermit Warbler
    • Williamson's Sapsucker
    • Purple Finch

  • Grassland

    • Western Meadowlark
    • Savannah Sparrow
    • Western Kingbird

  • Sagebrush Steppe

    • Sharp-tailed Grouse
    • Sage Thrasher
    • Brewer's Sparrow

Look for these birds the next time you visit one of these habitats in the Pacific Northwest
